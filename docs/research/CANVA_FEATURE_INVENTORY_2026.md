# Every Canva Content-Creation Feature: The Exhaustive List

This is a granular, itemized inventory of Canva's content-creation capabilities, grouped into logical categories. It reflects features current as of mid-2026, including launches from Canva Create 2025 (Visual Suite 2.0), the October 2025 "Creative Operating System" release, and Canva Create 2026 (Canva AI 2.0). Where a feature's availability or rollout is limited or uncertain, that is noted inline.

## TL;DR
- Canva spans the full content-creation stack: 100+ design/content types, a complete AI suite (Magic Studio + Canva AI 2.0), photo and multi-track video editing, audio, typography, elements, animation, branding, data/spreadsheets, websites, print, publishing/scheduling, collaboration, apps/integrations, and the now-free Affinity professional suite.
- The biggest recent additions are conversational AI (Canva AI 2.0 with the world's-first Canva Design Model), Canva Sheets, Canva Code, Canva Email Design, Canva Forms, Magic Charts, Magic Studio at Scale, Video 2.0, and a fully AI-powered Photo Editor.
- This list itemizes every individual capability found; some newest features (e.g., Canva AI 2.0 workflows, Magic Layers) are in research-preview or staged regional rollout.

## Key Findings
Canva positions itself not as a graphic-design tool but as a "Creative Operating System" — launched October 2025 as "our biggest product launch to date" — that "brings together a supercharged Visual Suite, world-first design AI, plus powerful ways to grow your business and scale your brand." It is framed as three pillars: the **Visual Suite** (all design types), **Canva AI** (generative tools), and the **Platform** (brand, marketing, developer tools). Content-creation features fall into the categories itemized below.

## Recommendations
- **For the deliverable document:** use the categorized list above verbatim; it is organized for direct conversion into a report. Each bullet is a discrete, itemized feature as requested.
- **Verify regional/plan availability** for the newest items before publishing to end users: Canva AI 2.0 (research preview, launched April 2026), Magic Layers (US/UK/Canada/Australia only), popular music library (select regions), and Video 2.0 (beta). These are the items most likely to differ by account.
- **Flag plan gating:** Many AI/Magic tools, premium music, Brand Controls, Content Planner, and custom domains require Canva Pro/Teams/Enterprise/Education; note this if the audience is on the Free plan.
- **Treat third-party-sourced specifics cautiously:** Exact animation preset names should be confirmed against the live Canva UI if precision matters; the marketplace app count (430 as of end-2024) will have grown since.

## Caveats
- Canva ships features continuously; this reflects the state through mid-2026 and some items are in beta or staged rollout.
- The marketplace app count is a moving figure — Canva Developers' 2024 "Wrapped" reported 430 apps at the close of 2024; the live count is higher today.
- Specific animation preset name lists are corroborated by multiple tutorials but Canva's own enumerated list was not directly accessible; names may vary by element type.
- A few commonly cited integrations (Giphy, YouTube embed, Emoji, Typeform) could not be confirmed on a first-party Canva URL during research and are omitted or should be verified.
- AI copyright/ownership terms vary by jurisdiction; commercial use is permitted under Canva's AI terms but exclusive rights may not apply to AI-generated outputs.
