# Adobe Express: Complete Content-Creation Feature Inventory

This is an exhaustive, categorized inventory of every content-creation feature in Adobe Express (formerly Adobe Spark), the web, mobile, and PWA "create-anything" app. Free (F) vs Premium (P) is noted where Adobe documents it. Items marked (AI) are powered by Adobe Firefly or partner AI models. Coverage is current to the February 2026 release notes.

## TL;DR
- Adobe Express offers hundreds of content-creation features across templates, image editing, video editing, audio, generative AI (Firefly), text/typography, PDF/documents, drawing, design elements, branding, scheduling/publishing, collaboration, and one-click Quick Actions — spanning a Free tier and a Premium tier.
- The 2025–2026 push is overwhelmingly AI: the conversational AI Assistant (beta), Generate Image/Video/Template/Text Effects, Generative Fill/Expand/Remove, Translate/dub, Generate Speech voiceover, Clip Maker, and Dynamic physics animation.
- This document is a pure feature list as requested; every capability below is a discrete content-creation feature.

## Key Findings (Feature Inventory)

### 1. Templates & Design Types
- Thousands of professionally designed, fully customizable templates — Adobe's Express for Teams page states it lets you "Build better content from 220,000+ professional templates and millions of Adobe Stock assets"
- Template categories: social media posts, Instagram posts/stories/reels, TikTok videos, Facebook posts/stories, YouTube videos/thumbnails/banners, Pinterest pins, X (Twitter) posts
- Flyers, posters, banners, logos, invitations, cards (birthday, holiday, thank you), business cards (standard, vertical, virtual/digital, visiting), resumes, cover letters
- Brochures, menus, pamphlets, leaflets, infographics, certificates, worksheets, class schedules
- Book covers, album covers, product labels, gift certificates, ads, memes, photo collages, wallpapers (desktop/phone), t-shirts
- Presentations, documents, web pages, carousels
- Animated templates; multi-page templates; video templates
- Print-ready templates; portrait, landscape, square, and vertical orientations
- Blank canvas (start from scratch) with custom dimensions
- Save any project as a reusable/shareable template; Favorites for saving templates and assets
- Quick replace to customize templates fast
- Brand-controlled / locked templates with style restrictions (Teams/Enterprise)

### 2. AI / Generative Features (Adobe Firefly–powered)
- **Generate Image** (Text to Image) — text prompt to images; art styles, effects, color/tone, lighting, camera angle; reference image upload; "Show Similar"; 100+ language prompt support (AI)
- **Generate Text Effect** — apply AI textures/styles to text/letters from a prompt (AI)
- **Text to Template** (Generate Template) — generate fully editable templates from a prompt for social posts, flyers, posters, etc. (AI)
- **Generative Fill** — insert, remove, or replace objects/people via brush + prompt (AI)
- **Insert or replace objects** with generative AI (AI)
- **Remove objects** with generative AI (AI)
- **Generative Expand / Expand image** — extend image to new dimensions, AI fills new area (P, AI)
- **Generate Similar / Generate similar images** — produce on-style variations (AI)
- **Generate Video** (Firefly Video Model) — text + image prompts to b-roll/background footage; shot size, camera angle, camera settings (AI)
- **Generate presentation** — slide decks from prompts or uploaded documents (AI)
- **Generate coloring pages** — text prompt to printable/colorable line art (AI)
- **Generate captions** / Caption Writer for social posts (AI)
- **Generate QR code** (also listed under tools)
- **Rewrite / text variations** — AI text rewriting and variations (AI)
- **Translate** designs into many languages, incl. regional variants (AI)
- **Generate Speech / AI voiceover** (Firefly Speech) — the Adobe Firefly AI Voice Generator page lets you "Select a voice from 45 Firefly options," and Adobe's Text to Speech page describes the full library as "over 70 high-quality voices powered by Adobe and trusted partners like ElevenLabs" across "more than 20 languages"; controls for pitch/speed/emotion/tone and fix pronunciation; download as WAV (AI)
- **Translate Video / Translate Audio (AI dubbing)** — per Slator (reporting Adobe), the feature lets users "translate audio files and dub videos into up to five target languages per file," preserving the original voice, within Firefly's 20+ language support; optional Lip Sync for enterprise (AI; via Firefly)
- **Text to Avatar / AI avatars** — studio-grade talking avatar videos (AI)
- **AI Assistant (beta)** — conversational creation/editing; generate images, change backgrounds/text, replace objects, position/align/stylize, edit individual layers; voice commands via microphone; uses Firefly + partner models (Gemini 2.5 Flash Image, Flux.1 Kontext, Google Gemini 2.5 Flash); toggle on/off; smart prompt suggestions; Imaging Subagent for AI image editing/compositing (beta; desktop)
- **Enhance Speech** — removes background noise from audio (AI)
- **Clip Maker** — AI turns long video into short social clips with captions and reframing (AI)
- Font recommendations (AI-assisted) and music recommendations
- Content Credentials attached to AI-generated content; commercially safe Firefly models
- Generative credits system — per Adobe's Generative Credits FAQ free credits "expire one month from that allocation date"; free Adobe ID users receive a monthly allocation (commonly 25 generative credits/month) rather than a perpetual daily pool

### 3. Image / Photo Editing
- **Remove background** (one-click) (P; free for a limited time per Adobe)
- **Resize image** — presets for social platforms + custom dimensions
- **Crop image** — freeform, aspect ratios, social presets
- **Crop into shapes** — circle, heart, star, oval, square, triangle, etc.
- **Convert image formats** — JPG, PNG, SVG, WebP (e.g., PNG↔JPG, JPG/PNG→SVG, WebP→JPG/PNG)
- **Erase** tool — brush/quick-select removal of unwanted parts (P)
- **Photo filters** — 8 primary filter styles with many variations; Shuffle; intensity slider; 30+ Photoshop-powered filters
- **Adjustments** — contrast, brightness, highlights, shadow, saturation, warmth, sharpen
- **Effects** — duotone, grayscale, blur, opacity, blend modes, golden hour, matte B&W, color punch
- **Photo collages** — manual, templates, or preset grid layouts
- **Frames and overlays**; crop & shape (rotate, scale, nudge, flip)
- **Create profile pictures**
- **Enhance** in a snap; animated images (Spin, Pop, Jitter, Slide, Zoom, Pan, Wobble, Wind, color/fade/blur animations)
- **Replace images**; set image as page background; replace background
- **Image enhancement** via AI Assistant (pose change, object swap, distraction removal)
- HEIC image import support

### 4. Video Editing
- Multi-track video editor with timeline; layer timing; adjust layer timing; locate timed objects
- Edit multiple videos in one file; Scene view (reorder, batch-edit); Drop Zone (compile clips into sequences)
- **Trim video**, **Crop video**, **Resize video** (social presets + custom)
- **Merge / combine videos**
- **Change video speed** (slow motion to super-fast presets); **Reverse video**; **Mute video**
- **Convert video to GIF**; **Convert to MP4**; GIF→MP4, GIF→Video conversions; download videos/animations as GIF
- **Remove background from video** (no green screen) (P); restore background
- **Caption video / auto captions** — editable, 100+ languages; in-editor auto captions (desktop); reposition captions
- **Video transitions**; **animations**; **layer objects** (text, photos, design elements)
- **Add audio to video**; voiceover; **video self-record** (webcam)
- Slip Edit, scene trim/extend with snapping; collapse audio tracks

- **Clip Maker** (AI long-to-short, captions, reframing — desktop, English)
- Export/publish directly to Vimeo (add-on)

### 5. Audio Features

- Add sound effects (library)

### 6. Text & Typography

## Recommendations
- Present sections 1–19 as the categorized feature list — this is the pure inventory the request calls for. If a single master list is needed, lead with Quick Actions (Section 16) and Templates (Section 1) as the most-used entry points.
- Flag AI Assistant, Brand Check, and Express API as beta/rolling-out so the document stays accurate over time; revisit after each monthly release-notes update (the feature set changes monthly).
- Before publishing externally, re-verify the font count (Adobe cites 20,000 / 22,000 / 25,000 / 28,000 on different pages) and confirm current Free-vs-Premium status of Remove Background, since Adobe describes it as "free for a limited time."
- Thresholds that would change this list: a new monthly release-notes entry (add features), Adobe moving AI Assistant to general availability (remove the beta flag), or any change to the generative-credit allocation model.

## Caveats
- Free vs Premium status changes frequently (e.g., Remove Background "free for a limited time"; Erase and video background removal are Premium).
- AI Assistant is a limited beta (desktop, select languages: English, French, German, Japanese, Hindi, Brazilian Portuguese; personal accounts only).
- Some quick actions (Reverse video, Change Speed) are surfaced as feature pages rather than core SDK methods; Mute is usually an in-tool toggle rather than a standalone tile.
- Asset, font, and template counts vary across Adobe pages and over time; figures above are quoted from Adobe/Adobe Stock sources at the time of research.
- Generative-credit allocations are monthly (not daily) and expire one month after allocation per Adobe's Generative Credits FAQ; specific numbers depend on plan.
- Lip Sync in Translate Video is limited to enterprise customers with eligible Firefly plans; AI voiceover via WellSaid/AiVOOV requires those third-party add-on accounts.
