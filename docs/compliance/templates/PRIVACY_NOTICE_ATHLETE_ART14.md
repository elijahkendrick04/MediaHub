## Who we are

**[Club name]**, contact: **[club email]**. We are the "controller" — the
club decides what happens with your information. We use **MediaHub**
(operated by **[operator name]**) as our tool; it stores and processes the
information for us under a contract.

## What we collect about you (or your child)

- **From meet results files** (entered by our team after a competition):
  name, age group/year of age on the day, sex category, club, events,
  times, and placings.
- **From public Swim England rankings (swimmingresults.org)** — this is
  the part we don't get from you: your published personal-best history
  (event, time, date), which we look up so we can tell when a swim is a
  new personal best. *[Delete this bullet if the club has switched PB
  enrichment off.]*
- **Photos** that the club uploads (for example race photos), if your
  family has given them to the club or a club photographer took them.

## What we use it for

To celebrate swims: we create social-media posts — result cards, athlete
spotlights, meet recaps — about achievements like personal bests and
medals. **A human at the club reviews and approves every post before it
goes anywhere.**

## Saying no — and changing your mind

- You can tell us **not to feature you** at any time: tell **[contact]**
  or use the complaint/contact form at **[MediaHub /complaints URL]**.
- A "no" takes effect immediately for all future posts — the system
  physically blocks making cards about you.
- For under-18s we ask a parent/guardian; clubs using consent mode will
  not post about an under-18 without a recorded parental yes.

## Who else sees the data

- **AI captioning services** (Google Gemini; Anthropic as backup) receive
  the achievement facts (name, event, time) to draft the caption text —
  under contracts that forbid them using it to train their AI.
- **Photo processing** *[if enabled]*: photos may be processed by
  [Photoroom/Replicate] to cut out backgrounds.
- **Our hosting provider** ([e.g. Render], in the United States, under
  UK-approved safeguards) stores the data.
- **Social media platforms** (Instagram/Facebook/TikTok): once the club
  approves a post, the platform processes it under its own rules.
- Full, current list: **[MediaHub /legal/subprocessors URL]**.

## How long we keep it

Raw results files: up to **[180]** days. Posts and cards: up to **[2
years / club setting]**. Personal-best lookups: up to 30 days. Old data
is deleted automatically on a schedule.

## Your rights

You (or your parent/guardian for you) can ask us to: **see** everything we
hold about you; **fix** anything wrong; **delete** you from our content
system; **pause** ("restrict") use of your data; or **object** to it. Ask
**[contact]** — we must respond within a month. You can also complain to
us at **[MediaHub /complaints URL]** (we acknowledge within 30 days) or go
directly to the regulator, the ICO: ico.org.uk, 0303 123 1113.
