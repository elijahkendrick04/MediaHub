# frame_breakout

**Family / structural signature.** The "pop-out" announcement composition: the
athlete cutout is clipped inside a brand shape frame (a lit circle by default,
an arch variant) sitting on a depth-shadowed disc, while a second, pixel-aligned
paint of the *same* cutout escapes above the frame's top edge — the head or
raised arm breaks the mask. Two copies from one `--mh-athlete-img` source, each
`inset:0` of the same square stage, so they align by construction; the inside
copy is clipped to the disc, the breakout copy to only the band above it. Below
the frame, a centred fact plane carries the first name, the mega surname, the
event, the pill result and the club lockup. Distinct from `spotlight_disc` (the
subject stays fully inside the circle) and `poster_name_behind` (type behind the
subject).

**When the director should pick it** when there is a strong, clean single-athlete
cutout and the moment deserves a hero announcement — a debut, a call-up, a
standout individual swim — where the subject breaking out of a branded medallion
reads as deliberate art direction rather than a plain photo window. Needs a
cutout-friendly photo (head and shoulders clear of the frame); without one the
disc becomes a clean brand emblem and the fact plane still anchors the card.
