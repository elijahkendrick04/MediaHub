# photo_passepartout

The athlete photo is hung like a framed print: a generous brand **mat**
(passe-partout) surrounds a windowed image with a thin accent keyline, and a
caption plate beneath the window carries the name, event and result like a
gallery label. The matted window plus caption plate is the structural signature —
a calm, premium, exhibition feel distinct from the edge-to-edge full-bleed stage
and the hard diagonal wedge. With no photo the window holds a clean brand panel,
so the framing still reads.

**When the director should pick it:** considered, premium portrait moments — a
season spotlight, a captain or milestone profile, a club-of-the-month feel — where
restraint and a gallery register suit the brand better than full-bleed drama. Best
with a strong vertical portrait; suits "calm", "warm", "stoic" and editorial
registers. Avoid for fast hype moments (use the scorebug or diagonal hero) or when
there is no decent photo to frame.
